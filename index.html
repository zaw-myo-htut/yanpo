YNPO Resume

              Hello, I'm Yan Paing, also known as, Yanpo, originally from Myanmar and currently residing in Thailand. With a background in business, my passion for graphic design ignited in early 2020, leading me to embark on a freelance journey in 2021. During this time, I crafted logo identities for several SMEs.

                Eager to expand my designer career, I seized the opportunity to delve into UI/UX design as an intern at FN in 2022. Over the course of two years at FN, I honed my craft, producing visually captivating websites and applications that prioritize both functionality and user experience, effectively conveying business narratives. Transitioning to NI, I immersed myself in Japanese projects, focusing on striking visual design while ensuring product usability remains paramount.

                After spending a couple of years in the design field, I've come to realize the significant impact it has on everyday lives and society at large. That’s why, my ultimate goal is to create exceptional products that not only foster business growth but also enrich the lives of users and contribute positively to society and the environment.
